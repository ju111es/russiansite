Stress Marks

This site uses bold text to indicate where stress falls in words. Stress is the part of the word pronounced the most- for example, the stress falls on the first a in the words Basics, Cases, and Vocabulary. Stress always falls on a vowel and is very important in Russian grammar. Remember, ё is always stressed :)

A note on gender

Russian is a very gendered and binary language, and it can be difficult to use for someone who doesn't feel comfortable with such terms. Transgender and non-binary native Russian speakers of course exist; some non-binary Russian speakers choose to use the neuter grammar while some prefer to switch between masculine and feminine, especially since the neuter gender is generally for inanimate objects. However you choose to express yourself in Russian, this site is for everyone ❤
