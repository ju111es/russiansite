Stress Marks
This site uses bold text to indicate where stress falls in words. Stress is the part of the word pronounced the most- for example, the stress falls on the first a in the words Basics, Cases, and Vocabulary. Stress always falls on a vowel and is very important in Russian grammar. Remember, ё is always stressed :)
