Consonant Mutation

In Russian, some consonants mutate under certain circumstances, usually in certain verb conjugations. So,

Which words undergo consonant mutation?...

★ most -ать verbs

★ -ить and -еть second conjugation verbs

★ most adjectives

...And when?

-ать verbs
★ every conjugation
★ in the imperative mood

-ить and -еть verbs
★ first person singular conjugation only

adjectives
★ in comparative form (adjectives with mutating stems use the ending -е instead of -ее)

And finally, which consonants mutate?

consonant → mutation
б → бл
в → вл
м → мл
п → пл
ф → фл
т к → ч
д г з → ж
с х → ш
ст ск → щ

★ Consonants in pink
do not mutate in -ать
verb conjgations.

★ Consonants in bold
do not mutate in
adjectives.
