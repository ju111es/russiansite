Verbs of Motion

Verbs of motion are some of the most used verbs, and many of them are constructed from just two verb pairs:

indeterminate determinate
by foot ходить идти
by other means ездить ехать

★ Hover over verbs with dotted underline to see their full conjugations.

Indeterminate vs. Determinate

Determinate verbs of motion indicate motion in one direction. These verbs are also known as unidirectional.

Я сегодня еду в Москву.I'm going (by transport) to Moscow today. Мы едем на автобусе в кино.We are taking the bus to the theater. Она идёт в школу.She goes (by foot) to school.

Indeterminate verbs indicate basically everything determinate verbs don't: motion in more than one direction, or motions made habitually. These verbs are also known as multidirectional.

Я вчера ездила в Москву.I went (by transport) to Moscow yesterday.
a return trip is implied, making the movement multidirectional		 Мы ездим на автобусе по городу.We are taking the bus around town.
'around town' implies multidirectional movement		 Она ходит в школу каждый день.She goes (by foot) to school every day.
'every day' = habitual

Prefixes

Prefixes for verbs of motion specify direction of movement.

prefixmeaning
вinto
выout of
доas far as
заdropping in
наonto
обaround
отaway from
переacross
подapproaching
приarrival
проthrough; past; over a distance/time
сdown
уfrom; leaving
поperfective aspect


With a prefix, идти becomes йти (or ойти with prefixes ending in a consonant), and ездить becomes езжать. Also, because direction is specified by the prefix, all prefixed verbs of motion are determinate. Instead of indicating direction, the indeterminate verbs now function as the imperfective aspects, and determinate verbs as the perfective.

Сейчас 08:00. Я прихожу на работу. It is 08:00. I am arriving at work. Сейчас 16:00. Я ухожу с работы. It is 16:00. I am leaving work.
Сейчас 12:00. Я пришел на работу 4 часа назад и уйти с работы через 4 часа. It is 12:00. I arrived at work 4 hours ago and will leave work in 4 hours.

Examples

я вхожу в домI am walking into the house я отойду от домI will walk away from the house я приезжаю в домI am arriving at the house (by transport) я уеду с домаI will leave the house (by transport)

Notice that many of these prefixed verbs are used with prepositions, most commonly в + accusative case or с + genitive case.

Some prefixes, namely про, can express multiple differrent concepts:

Я бы прошла 100 километров!I would walk 100 kilometers!
the prefix indicates motion over a certain distance		 Они проехали домой за четыре часа.They got home in four hours (it took four hours to get there).
the prefix indicates a motion/route completed over a certain time
Быстрее пройти через лес.It's faster to go though the forest.
the prefix indicates motion through, along with через		 Смотри, мы проезжаем мимо реки!Look, we're going past a river!
the prefix indicates motion past

По always indicates the perfective aspect of any verb of motion.
